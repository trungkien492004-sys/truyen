Chapter 384 Lord
After the order is issued.
Zhu Jun's team was the first to make a move.
Under the cover of night, campfires flickered in the bounty hunters' camp.
Qin Feng leaped down from the tree.
He bypassed several lookout bounty hunters and headed straight for Theodore's back.
According to his plan, he would slap the back of the neck, knock the person unconscious, and then carry them away.
Simple.
clean.
Just as Qin Feng raised his hand, Theodore suddenly turned his head.
Their eyes met.
Qin Feng's hand froze in mid-air.
His eyes became vacant, and his body stopped moving.
Theodore was pale and sweat beaded on his forehead.
"Don't come any closer."
He looked around, his voice trembling slightly.
"I don't want to hurt you."
Qin Feng stood still, slowly lowered his hand, and then turned around to block Theodore's path.
The bounty hunters in the camp then realized what was happening.
"who?"
"Enemies!"
Several guns were raised.
Zhu Jun emerged from the woods.
He didn't rush to make a move, but instead glanced at Theodore.
"Can you hear what other people are thinking in advance?"
Theodore's chest heaved rapidly.
He controlled Qin Feng, making Qin Feng stand in front of him.
Why are you arresting me?
Zhu Jun said, "I'll help you get rid of this burden."
"I'm not going."
Theodore's tone was filled with fear and exhaustion.
He has had a very painful and difficult time these past few years.
He can hear too much.
The father's greed.
Malice from a companion.
The prisoners' pleas.
And there's also the cowardice in my own heart.
The more he tries to avoid it, the more he can't escape it.
Zhu Jun walked forward step by step.
Theodore's expression changed.
His spiritual power directly impacted Zhu Jun's mind.
The next moment, he swayed.
He saw many scenes.
fire.
Blood.
Alien spaceship.
A white light that destroys the world.
A world where you start over again and again.
Another man led a group of people, charging forward like madmen.
Zhu Jun died many times and woke up many times in those memories.
Sometimes, he would be pulled back into hibernation shortly after opening his eyes.
Sometimes, he would emerge from the hibernation pod only to find himself already on a new battlefield.
His memory is very full.
So much so that Theodore couldn't distinguish which part was real, which part was the past, and which part was the future.
Most importantly, Zhu Jun's heart was not empty.
He was afraid of dying.
It gets tiring too.
They also get annoyed by Wang Cong's unethical ideas.
But if it's time to go, he will go.
Theodore's lips turned pale, and he took a step back.
"Why...is your heart?"
Zhu Jun did not answer.
He had already walked up to Theodore.
Qin Feng was still being controlled and raised his hand to try to stop them.
Zhu Jun kicked Qin Feng aside.
Qin Feng fell to the ground, his head swayed, and he finally woke up.
"Holy crap!"
He had just uttered a curse when he saw Zhu Jun raise his hand and punch Theodore in the face.
Theodore's eyes rolled back and he collapsed.
Zhu Jun caught him and tied him up with a rope.
Several bounty hunters raised their guns, ready to fire.
Qin Feng was furious and had nowhere to vent his anger, so he turned around and rushed over.
A moment later, the camp fell silent.
Qin Feng walked back, rubbing his temples.
"Brother Zhu, this kid is a bit strange. I was just thinking about how to knock him out, and he already knew everything."
Zhu Jun carried Theodore on his back.
So you're overthinking it.
Qin Feng: "..."
...
Fang Yuan was not at the mine when he received the news of the operation.
He was in a very remote cave.
This cave was originally an abandoned mine shaft, which he later converted into a temporary experimental site.
The entrance to the cave was covered by vines and wooden planks.
From the outside, it looks like a jumble of rocks.
When Qi Xia walked in, the smell inside was very strong.
He frowned.
"Captain, we've been ordered to arrest someone."
Fang Yuan was squatting in front of a wooden table, holding a very thin iron needle in his hand.
There were many bottles and jars on the table, and several black worms crawling around.
He didn't turn around immediately.
"knew."
Qi Xia couldn't help but ask, "Where's the target? Where's Allen? I haven't seen him for over half a year!"
Fang Yuan pointed to the darkest corner of the cave.
"There it is!"
Qi Xia walked a little further in.
Along the way, various metals were piled up on the ground.
blade.
Iron nails.
barrel.
horseshoe.
There were even a few coins.
Some of these things were twisted into ropes, some were pressed into thin sheets, and some were cut into very fine strips.
At the end of the road, Qi Xia saw it!
There, a figure was bound by countless translucent silk threads, suspended in mid-air, its body covered with thin silver needles, and its body twitched from time to time.
It was Allen, the one who could control metal.
But now, he was no longer the arrogant man he once was. He was so thin that he was emaciated, and his eyes were empty, like a puppet whose soul had been taken away.
Qi Xia felt a chill run down her spine.
At this moment, Fang Yuan had already arrived beside Qi Xia without anyone noticing.
"Take him outside and wash him, it'll be fine!"
Qi Xia shivered!
At this moment, Allen weakly raised his head, stared at Fang Yuan, and said with hatred in his eyes, "You will be punished."
Fang Yuan put a Gu worm into his mouth.
"If retribution can be consistently reproduced, it is also worth studying."
Qi Xia: "..."
...
In front of the log cabin at the logging camp.
Wang Cong was squatting in the grass, looking at Uncle Wolf's house in the distance.
Junming squatted down next to him, mimicking his posture, and remained motionless.
"Master, what exactly are we observing?" Junming asked in a low voice.
Wang Cong answered seriously, "Observe the enemy's fighting style!"
Wang Cong had already used his mental power to carve a small hole in the wooden house, and both Wang Cong and Jun Ming could see inside.
Junming asked in bewilderment, "Their fighting style is so inefficient!"
Wang Cong instructed, "You've only seen the first layer. Physical combat is one aspect, but mental combat is the key!"
Junming: "Do they have a mental fighting spirit?"
"That's the beauty of this battle. In a moment, this woman will completely submit to this man..."
Junming nodded as if he understood, thinking his master was amazing.
Just then, Zhang Ran walked back from the path in the distance.
Wang Cong's eyes narrowed, and he immediately stood up from the bushes.
"action!"
Junming was stunned: "Master, didn't you say an hour later?"
Wang Cong patted him on the shoulder and said earnestly, "Plans are rigid, but people are flexible. Opportunities in battle are fleeting; you must know how to adapt to changing circumstances. My apprentice, you should learn from this!"
Junming looked at Wang Cong's back, which had suddenly become unfathomable, and felt a deep sense of respect.
Master is indeed Master!
Even though nothing unexpected happened, the ability to anticipate the need for "adaptability" is an insight that I could never learn in my entire life.
Wang Cong had no time to pay attention to his apprentice's wild imagination.
He flashed forward, carrying Junming, and rushed straight to the wooden house, kicking open the door.
Inside the house, Wolf was embracing his girlfriend, and the two had just rolled around halfway down the stairs.
Wang Cong's sudden intrusion stunned the two of them.
After realizing what had happened, the two immediately started getting dressed.
"Junming!" Wang Cong shouted without turning his head, "We'll each take one of them! Remember, capture them alive!"
Junming rushed in right behind him, and upon hearing the order, he instinctively lunged at the obviously more threatening Wolf Uncle.
Wang Cong grabbed him.
"What are you thinking? Go deal with that woman!"
Wolf Uncle finally realized what was happening. Looking at the two intruders and then at his frightened girlfriend in his arms, a beastly rage erupted from his eyes.
He roared, clenched his fist, and his bone claws were about to pierce through his flesh.
However, the next second, an overwhelming, invisible force suddenly descended!
Wang Cong's mental strength was like a mountain, pressing down heavily on Uncle Wolf.
Wolfman's muscles bulged and veins stood out as he tried to break free of the restraints, only to find himself seemingly encased in cement, unable to move even a finger.
Junming, on the other hand, was in a bit of a flurry of activity.
He rarely captured people alive, for fear that he might kill them if he used too much strength.
So he and the woman wrestled together.
Just then, Zhang Ran arrived at the door.
She could see the scene inside the room at a glance.
Wang Cong casually "controlled" the immobilized Wolf Uncle with one hand in his pocket.
Junming was holding a woman whose breasts were exposed.
"Wang Cong! This is the apprentice you taught!"
Wang Cong laughed heartily: "Physiology class is a class too!"
...
The arrest operation went smoothly.
The only team that encountered a minor mishap was Jiang Yi's team.
It's not because of their strong combat power.
It's not because that woman is too good at running away.
She can become different people.
elder.
man.
Soldier.
A village woman.
Jiang Yi couldn't bring himself to be ruthless towards the elderly, weak, sick, and disabled, so he let them escape time and time again.
In the final instance, the shapeshifter transformed into a little girl and blended into a group of children, causing Jiang Yi to completely lose sight of her.
Fang Yuan, who had finished his mission ahead of schedule and happened to be passing by, made his move.
Only then did they capture the shapeshifter and take her back.
...
In the temporary base.
All the captured “evil forces” were brought back, bound tightly, and lined up in a row.
Jiang Yi was still somewhat dejected.
He walked up to Fang Yuan and couldn't help but ask, "Captain Fang, how did you figure that out? I really thought she was just a child."
Fang Yuan glanced at him and said calmly, "Children don't fear death."
Jiang Yi didn't understand. He thought it was some kind of pheromone or some other detail, but it turned out to be "not afraid of death"!
What does this have to do with finding someone?
He wanted to ask something else.
Wang Cong waved his hand, interrupting him: "Alright, stop asking."
At this moment, Fang Yuan raised his hand and handed something to Wang Cong.
It was a black pendant strung on a leather cord.
"Sect Leader, look at this."
Wang Cong took it and was immediately stunned.
He was all too familiar with the shape of that pendant.
It was a square stele!
It's the monument that grants players special privileges!
"Where did this thing come from?" Wang Cong's voice changed.
"Found it on that shapeshifting woman," Fang Yuan replied.
Wang Cong suddenly turned his head and looked at the bound woman. At this moment, she had returned to her original appearance, a blue-clad woman with a perfect figure.
What happened?
After more than a year of observation, he was 100% certain that these people were natives of this instance world who had awakened superpowers for some special reason, or rather, mutated, and were not players who had descended upon the world.
But... how could a native possess a privilege item exclusive to high-dimensional players?
Wang Cong wanted to put it into his watch, but he couldn't.
The Little Genius Watch is not a spatial ring; it can only hold dungeon rewards and has no ability to hold other items.
How did Rose originally mark her own tombstone?
Wang Cong couldn't remember how Rose had given him the first memorial tablet.
However, the second time Rose granted him access, it must have come at a terrible cost.
Therefore, this thing is definitely not something you can just pick up and use casually.
Wang Cong held the cold, square-shaped pendant in his hand, feeling that he had many more questions now.
If I can't figure it out, I'll just stop thinking about it.
Wang Cong carefully put away the square tablet and glanced at the row of arrested "X-Men" in front of him.
Ten days have passed since the order to close in.
Ten squads brought back ten mutated "evil forces"!
Wolverine, Theodore, Ellen, Transformers...
Wang Cong began:
"Junming said he saw me helping them in the dungeon."
"I've decided to verify it myself."
Wang Cong looked at Fang Yuan: "Wake these people up in a bit, then I'll open the barrier of this instance and put on the mask!"
Then, he solemnly instructed, "Fang Yuan, you are responsible for getting me out of the barrier immediately after I lose consciousness for ten seconds!"
Fang Yuan nodded: "Understood."
The group began to awaken these mutated "evil forces".
They opened their eyes blankly, looking at the unfamiliar surroundings and unfamiliar people, their eyes filled with wariness and confusion.
Wang Cong no longer hesitated.
He flew into the air and, as expected, was blocked after reaching a certain altitude.
This time it's not a void layer, it's just a regular dungeon barrier.
Bobo and the Heaven-Slaying Sword were drawn at the same time.
The Heaven-Slaying Sword is more obedient, but Bobo is not so obedient.
Fortunately, Bobo's strength was suppressed in the historical instance, and it soon had no choice but to obey.
"Po~"
With a single sword strike, the sky seemed to be cleaved open like a pane of glass, creating a massive rift.
Beyond the rift lies not the familiar starry sky, but a chaotic, distorted, bizarre, and unknown space.
"What kind of hell is this place out there?"
Could the outside of the historical replica be the river of time?
Wang Cong didn't have time to look closely.
The healing time for the instance barrier is only a few tens of seconds.
Then, in front of everyone, Wang Cong took out the painted face mask that belonged to Zhuanxu.
Under everyone's watchful eyes, he slowly put the mask on his face.
The moment the mask touched his face, Wang Cong's body stiffened abruptly, then he fell straight backward and instantly lost consciousness.
Fang Yuan timed it perfectly. Ten seconds after Wang Cong collapsed, he scooped him up in his arms, soared into the sky, and headed straight for the closing crack in the sky.
Just as Fang Yuan was carrying Wang Cong and was about to pass through the barrier...
On the ground, the mutants who had just woken up and were still in a hostile state all raised their heads.
His gaze swept over everyone and precisely locked onto the figure wearing a white mask who was being held in Fang Yuan's arms.
Confusion, vigilance, anger... all the complex emotions faded from their eyes in an instant.
Instead, an expression of profound familiarity and excitement appeared on his face.
The next second.
In unison, they used all their strength to shout a resounding cry towards the disappearing figure in the sky.
"host!"
